FuturICT Definition FuturICT was an EU FET Flagship (2011–2014 proposal phase) aimed at building a “Living Earth Simulator” and a “Global Participatory Platform” to model complex socio-economic-environmental systems. Key References Helbing et al. (2012), “FuturICT: Participatory Computing for Our Complex World”  ; Project archives: [futurict.eu](http://futurict.eu) Shares methodology goals with [[HISCL_core_goal]]: **participatory modelling**, global simulations, and socio-economic redesign.  Precursor to modern [[digital_twins]] approaches for sustainability.  Proof that large-scale *conceptual + participatory + computational* projects can gain EU-level traction.
